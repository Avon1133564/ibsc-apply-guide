How to Apply to IBSC — Yuan Ze University

For students from Vietnam 🇻🇳 | Step-by-step guide to join the International Bachelor Program in Strategic Communication

Phase 1 – Before You Apply (Planning)

  • Research your program: IBSC at Yuan Ze University
  • Check entry requirements (grades, English test scores)
  • Decide: Do you meet the requirements? If not, prepare for tests or explore other programs

Phase 2 – The Application (Action)

  • Create your account at Yuan Ze Application Portal
  • Fill out the online form completely
  • Upload all required documents: More Details
    • High school diploma
    • Official transcripts
    • Passport copy
    • Proof of English proficiency (IELTS / TOEFL / CEFR)
    • Personal statement
    • Financial statement (for international applicants)
  • Pay the application fee Check Here
  • Submit before deadline (check Yuan Ze Portal for updates)

Phase 3 – After You Apply (Next Steps)

  • Check your application result through email
  • If accepted → Accept offer → Register classes → Apply for visa (if needed)
  • If rejected → Contact admissions or explore other programs
